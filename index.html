How did I start?


I started my journey when I was a little 9 year old, at that time i was seeking to mischiviousness. Before I became a back-end programmer, I used to have a really really strong dream of becoming a professional hacker and exploiter.

My little self thus started searching around the internet, i remember how i typed "how to hack" in my bedroom's android TV while imagining myself hacking through complicated systems.

And how I would lie in front of my friends saying that I can code, and I've made my own game. When they asked me where my game is, I would open the game I donwloaded and say "I made this" (i didn't). Although it seems that dream turned into something better.
